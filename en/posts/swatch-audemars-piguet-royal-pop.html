Swatch x Audemars Piguet breaks the internet — with an unexpected twist

Swatch and Audemars Piguet have unveiled Royal Pop, a bright Pop Art pocket watch collection instead of the expected affordable Royal Oak-style release.

By Almir KhialovMay 13, 20265 minStyle

Swatch has officially introduced its long-awaited collaboration with Audemars Piguet, and the internet did not get what it expected. Many people were waiting for an accessible Royal Oak-inspired release, but the brands unveiled premium Pop Art pocket watches called Royal Pop.

What was shown

The collection includes eight bright models with AP’s octagonal language, Royal Oak references, a Bioceramic case and a hand-wound SISTEM51 movement.

The watches can be worn around the neck, attached to clothing, clipped to a bag or used as a visual accessory.

The internet expected something else

Social feeds were full of AI renders imagining a MoonSwatch-style Royal Oak, so many people expected a few-hundred-dollar AP-inspired watch. Swatch chose a stranger and more distinctive route.

Why it is still a huge release

After MoonSwatch, the market learned that people do not only buy luxury. They also buy access to a legend. Swatch keeps turning watch culture into mass internet culture.

Pereuloq view

Swatch is doing what it does best: turning a watch from a simple accessory into a pop-culture moment. In 2026, watch hype is clearly part of the larger internet again.
